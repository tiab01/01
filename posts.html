DEATH

5th September, 2021

'Death must be a beautiful journey because yet no one has ever come back' I wonder what it's like to be dead. Do we feel nothing? Will there be nothing after death? Will it all be dark all of a sudden and we will be left as nothing but an unfunctional body? Or will our soul fly high and enter another life just as in the myths? No one knows. Sometimes it's scary knowing how one day it will just be over. Suddenly, we will be gone. And yet sometimes it feels peaceful. Along with us goes our pain and miseries. For me death simply means giving up. Either we give up or in a long term our body does. When I think of death, I picture something dark, vacant and silent. While most of us fear to die, the thought of immortality scares me. A lifetime full of misery, despair and seeing people you love in pain and us not being able to be released from the cycle of life for eternity. We struggle all our lives, live the happy moments and the sad ones, make mistakes, learn from them and one day we die. Taking not-a-thing with us.
